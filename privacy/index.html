Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

Welcome to Edustream. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our educational platform, in compliance with the Sri Lanka Personal Data Protection Act, No. 9 of 2022.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, participate in courses, or communicate with us:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and contact details.
  • Account Data: Username and password credentials.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our videos and educational materials.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our educational services.
  • To personalize your learning experience.
  • To communicate updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Sri Lankan law.

3. Data Retention & Finality

As per our Terms and Conditions, we enforce strict data finality rules:

  • WINDOW Modification Window: You may edit or delete your data within one (1) day of registration. After this period, data modification requires administrative approval via Telegram.
  • DURATION Duration: We store your data securely until a formal website shutdown notice is issued by Edustream.

4. Disclosure of Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose your information only in the following cases:

  • Legal Requirements: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities in Sri Lanka.
  • Service Providers: To third-party companies that facilitate our service under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

Under the Sri Lanka Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the withdrawal of consent for processing (subject to the 1-day finality rule or manual admin review).
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority.

6. Security of Data

We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols. However, as stated in our Terms, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security against sophisticated cyber-attacks.
